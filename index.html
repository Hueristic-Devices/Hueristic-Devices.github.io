About

Hueristic Devices is a Bangalore based company with water and soil testing products.

The company was formed to take forward the research and product development work of Foundation for Environmental Monitoring

Products

HD Pro Water

A smartphone based product to test for key water parameters

HD Pro Soil

A smartphone based soil test kit

HD Elementary Water

A simple screening test for water

Spot Coli

A 30 minute test for Escheria Coli (E Coli) in water.
