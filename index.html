About

Hueristic Devices is a Bangalore based company with water and soil testing products.

The company was formed to take forward the research and product development work of Foundation for Environmental Monitoring

Products

HD Pro Water

An app-guided colorimetric water testing kit.
Portable.
Simple to use.
Immediate results.

Ideal for in situ and field testing, and for those who do not want to wait for their water test results. Pro Water colorimetric tests show a high correlation with lab results for fluoride, chlorine, iron, nitrate, pH, chromium, arsenic, phosphate, aluminum, hardness (calcium, magnesium), total alkalinity (carbonate, bicarbonates). TDS meters are also available.

HD Pro Soil

A portable app-based soil testing kit packaged for ergonomic use on field. Immediate results on soil analysis are important because soil is a metabolic medium and results vary with time. It is also important to be able to take decisions based on the results you get.

Pro Soil testing kits are immediate result soil test kits, optimized for quantitative results on soil macronutrients nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and for pH. Micronutrients are under development, write to us to know more.

New Procuts Under Development

HD Elementary Water

A simple, low-cost screening test for water, made for anyone who owns a smartphone and can take pictures with it.

Currently available for fluoride and pH. Iron, nitrate and phosphate under development.

HD Spot Coli

A 30-minute test for Escherichia coli, (E. coli) in water. Escherichia coli are normally found in the intestines of mammals and their presence is a sure indicator of fecal contamination of water. Not all E. coli are pathogenic.
