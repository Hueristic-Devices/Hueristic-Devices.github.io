About

Hueristic Devices is a Bangalore based company with water and soil testing products.

HD Pro Water

An app-guided colorimetric water testing kit. Portable. Simple to use. Immediate results.

Ideal for in situ and field testing, and for those who do not want to wait for their water test results. Pro Water colorimetric tests show a 95% to 99% correlation with lab results for fluoride, chlorine, iron, nitrate, pH, chromium, arsenic, phosphate, aluminum, hardness (calcium, magnesium), total alkalinity (carbonate, bicarbonates). TDS meters are also available.

FREE!! The Pro Water app that helps you manage the water data you collect, share it across teams, factory floors, with supervisors, collaborators and partner organizations. Data points can be geomapped, along with comments and description of sample collection points. Look out for our updates on data visualization maps, reports and dashboards.

HD Pro Soil

A portable app-based soil testing kit packaged for ergonomic use on field. Immediate results on soil analysis are important because soil is a metabolic medium and results vary with time. It is also important to be able to take decisions based on the results you get.

Pro Soil testing kits are immediate result soil test kits, optimized for quantitative results on soil macronutrients nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and for pH. Results show a 70% correlation with lab results. Micronutrients are under development, with organic carbon, boron, sulphur, YY, aa, bb, cc and zz available for early user testing. Your contribution to early testing experiments would be to share feedback, results, soil samples and geographic and topographic context with our R&D teams. We will give you discounts across parameters or time scales, as needed.

FREE!! Pro Soil app and visualization of soil health data on a map, on reports and shareable dashboards. Map snapshot status, progress, seasonal trends and share with communities and stakeholders.
